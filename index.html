My Heart, My Melody

In a world full of noise, you were the melody I never knew I was missing.

I’ve watched the stars, searched the sky, but none ever shined like the way your smile lights up the chaos in my mind.

We’ve shared laughter, late-night talks, and the quiet that only exists when two hearts truly see each other.

So here I am — not with lines rehearsed, but with truth in my chest…

Will you walk into the unknown with me, hand in hand, heart in sync?

Will you be mine — not just for a moment, but for every chapter after this?
